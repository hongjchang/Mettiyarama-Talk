    About This Page and The Event

       

    This webpage is created to compile and record the Buddhism talks presented by a Bhante from Mettiyarama Centre (a Buddhist organisation/meditation centre in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah).

    This talk is titled as "The Golden Key of Buddha" or "佛陀的经钥匙系列" in Chinese. It started since a year ago and is about "the truth of lifestyle" from Buddha's teachings which is based on the most traditional and original knowledges from the suttas.

    The organisation is based on Theravada Buddhism (or 南传/小乘佛教 in Chinese). Hence, the talk teaches about the truth of lifestyle based on Buddha's Teachings from (approx.) 2,500 years ago when The Buddha enlightened and gave teachings to the community. In a nutshell, the topics provided in this sessions are being a people with loving-kindness and other teachings for a more happier lifestyle.
