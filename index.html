    About This Page And Bhante's Talk

    This webpage is created to compile and record the Buddhism talks presented by a Bhante from Mettiyarama Centre (which is a Buddhist organisation / meditation centre). It is located in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah.

    The talks are an event called "The Golden Key of Buddha" or in Chinese "佛陀的经钥匙系列". It already started a year ago and it's about the Buddha's teachings which is based on the most traditional and actual knowledges.

    The organisation is based on Theravada Buddhism (or 南传/小乘佛教 in Chinese). Hence, this event (talk sessions) teaches about the real truth from 2,500 years ago where The Buddha enlightened and gave teachings to other people. In general, the topics provided in this sessions are being a people with loving-kindness and other teachings for a more happier lifestyle.
