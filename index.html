About This Website

This webpage is created to compile and record Bhante's talks from Mettiyarama Centre. It's a Theravada Buddhism-centred organisation and meditation retreat based in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah.

From 2022 till April 2023, the organisation hosted an online talk named "The Golden Key of Buddha" or "‰ΩõÈôÄÁöÑÁªèÈí•ÂåôÁ≥ªÂàó". It's about "the truth of our life" based from Buddha's teachings from about 2600 years ago when The Buddha enlightened and gave numerous teachings or Dhamma which passed down until now.

Next, starting from 2024, the organisation started a physical talk session event every Sunday fortnightly. The session recording was available on its social media site and are kinda presenting the contents in the aformentioned discontinued event from start.

In a nutshell, the Bhante's talk consists various topics according to The Buddha's teachings, in an easy-to-understand manner whereby newbies could know them easily. The talks centers around how to to create loving-kindness, practice a undiscriminated (wholesome) heart, and most importantly, understanding our lives that lead to no reincarnation/nibbana.
