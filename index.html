    About This Page And Event

         

    This webpage is created to compile and record the Buddhism talks presented by Bhante from Mettiyarama Centre. It's a Buddhist organisation and medication retreat based in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah.

    This talk is titled as "The Golden Key of Buddha" or "ä½›é™€çš„ç»é’¥åŒ™ç³»åˆ—" in Chinese. It started about one and half of the year ago and is about "the truth of lifestyle" from the Buddha's teachings in which it's based on the most traditional and original knowledges from the suttas.

    The organisation is based on Theravada Buddhism. Hence, the talk teaches about the truth of lifestyle based on Buddha's Teachings from (approx.) 2,500 years ago when The Buddha enlightened and gave numerous teachings to the community until his nibbana (kinda never reincarnated again).

    In a nutshell, this event is currently recurring and consists of various topics in the Buddha's Teachings, in which to make people create loving-kindness and practice a centric heart in our current life.

    Date: every Wednesday ℹ Every 2nd and 4th Wednesday of the month unless exceptions apply
    Time: 8:00pm (1 hour) ℹ Time Zone: GMT+8
    Venue: Zoom Meeting & Facebook Live ℹ Details will be published prior to nearly the start of each event instance.
